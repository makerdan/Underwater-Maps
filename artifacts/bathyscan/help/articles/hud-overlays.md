# HUD Overlay Toggles

The cluster of buttons in the **bottom-right of the HUD** toggles environmental
overlays directly on the 3D scene. They're always one click away so you can
quickly compare conditions without opening a side panel.

## Toggles

- **🔍 FIND DATA** — opens the [Find Data](#article:find-data) drawer.
- **◼ SUBSTRATE** — tints the seafloor by classified substrate type (sand,
  mud, gravel, rock). Helpful for habitat planning and pairing with the
  habitat layer.
- **💨 WIND** — overlays surface wind direction arrows. Colour intensity scales
  with wind speed.
- **🌊 TIDE** — overlays tidal-flow arrows. Pair with the
  [Tidal Overlay](#article:tidal-overlay) panel for height, slack windows,
  and time scrubbing.
- **↬ CURRENT** — overlays sub-surface current arrows. Use the Tidal Overlay
  panel's **Current layer** buttons to switch between surface, mid-column, and
  near-bottom layers.
- **🐟 ESSENTIAL FISH HABITAT** — (visible only for datasets bundled with
  Essential Fish Habitat data) overlays NOAA Essential Fish Habitat polygons
  coloured by species group.

## Tips

- Toggles are independent — leave wind on while you flip tide and current to
  compare layers.
- Each toggle's tooltip explains what it does on hover.
- The state of every toggle is remembered between sessions.
