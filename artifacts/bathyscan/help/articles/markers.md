# Markers

Markers are persistent pins you drop on the seafloor to remember a spot.

## Dropping a marker

![Press G, fill the Drop Marker form, save](/help/marker-drop.gif)

1. Enter **Fly mode** by clicking the scene.
2. Aim the crosshair at the target.
3. Press **G** or **right-click**.
4. The **Drop Marker** form appears with longitude, latitude, and depth pre-filled.
5. Pick a **Type**, fill in a **Label**, optionally add notes, and click **Save Marker**.

## Marker types

The available types change with the active water environment.

**Saltwater:** Fish school, Reef, Wreck, Hazard, Anchorage, Depth Pole, Custom.

**Freshwater:** Vegetation, Structure, Hazard, Launch, Depth Pole, Custom.

Each type has its own icon and colour so you can scan them at a glance.

## Depth poles

Choose **Depth Pole** when you want a tall vertical line that you can see from far away. You pick the colour at save time. Useful for marking a drop or a contour line you want to follow.

## Managing your markers

Open the **Markers** drop-down inside the Datasets panel to see every marker on the current dataset. From here you can:

- **Click** a marker to teleport to it (fly mode only).
- **Click ×** to delete it.

Markers are tied to the dataset — switching datasets shows a different list. Deleting a dataset deletes its markers too.

## Offline behaviour

If you drop a marker while offline, it is queued locally and uploaded as soon as you reconnect. You will see a yellow "Saved locally — will sync when online" banner in the form.
